Hero

The hero section in an web page serves as a visually striking introduction, often featuring captivating imagery or videos. Its primary purpose is to immediately engage visitors and convey the core message or purpose of the webpage. Typically located at the top, the hero section can include elements such as a headline, introductory text, and a prominent call-to-action button.
