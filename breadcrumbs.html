Breadcrumbs

Breadcrumbs are a navigational aid commonly used in websites to show users their current location within the website's hierarchy and provide a trail of the pages they have visited. They typically appear near the top of a webpage and consist of a series of links separated by a delimiter, such as a greater-than sign (>).
