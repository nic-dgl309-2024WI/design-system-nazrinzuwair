Our Journey

Outback Nursery, one of Vancouver Island's largest garden centers, sits on a 6-acre property boasting 5 spacious greenhouses (30x170ft) spanning almost 23,000 square feet. Established in 1998-1999, our journey began when our parents retired to the island from Aldergrove, inviting us to join them in purchasing this lush, tree-filled property. Initially delving into country life, we cleared trees, raised turkeys and pigs, and cultivated hedging and conifers. Our expansion saw us join the Farmer's Market and venture into retail, showcasing our quality products on island-wide trips. Gradually, our property evolved with increased greenhouses and irrigation zones, eventually opening our doors to the public, transitioning from wholesale to retail and landscape clientele.
