Carousel

A carousel is a web design element that displays multiple images or content slides in a rotating manner. It's often used to showcase products, promotions, or key information in a visually engaging way. Users can navigate through the carousel either manually or automatically, depending on the implementation. Carousels are popular for their ability to efficiently utilize space while offering dynamic and interactive content presentation.
