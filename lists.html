Lists

Lists in website design are essential elements for organizing and presenting information in a structured and easy-to-follow format. They can come in various types, including ordered lists (numbered), unordered lists (bulleted), and definition lists. Lists help break down content into digestible chunks, making it more scannable and comprehensible for users.
