Footer

The footer of a webpage is situated at the bottom and serves as a concluding section providing essential information and navigation links. It typically includes elements such as copyright notices, contact information, and links to important pages like privacy policy or terms of service. Social media icons may also be present, facilitating further engagement and connection with the website's audience. The footer contributes to the overall usability and professionalism of the website by offering easy access to supplementary information and enhancing user experience.
